"The Secret of Good Luck" is a captivating book that explores the essence of luck and success in life through a poignant story. It follows the encounter between two old men, Max and Jim, after 50 years apart. Despite their differing beginnings, Max finds success and happiness, while Jim struggles. Max shares a mystical story from his grandfather that holds the secrets to luck and success, guiding readers to understand that luck is not random; it requires intentional effort and the creation of favorable conditions. Written by Alex Rovira and Fernando Trías de Bes, the book offers profound insights into human attitudes and behaviors towards luck, inspiring readers to pursue their own paths to good fortune.
